Privacy Policy

How Decision Compass handles your data

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Privacy Linked Data

Decision Compass is a personal decision compass for self-reflection. It combines birth details, Eastern life-map logic, I Ching-inspired rituals, AI-guided interpretation, reports, and private journaling to help users reflect before important choices.

Who controls the data

Decision Compass is operated by Finnexion Oy. For privacy questions or data requests, contact info@finnexion.com.

Data we collect

  • Birth details you enter, such as birth date, optional birth time, city, and gender selection.
  • Optional precise location if you tap "Use current location" to set a birthplace.
  • App language, selected focus area, and onboarding profile choices.
  • Questions or focus text you enter for decision readings.
  • Generated profile and reading data, including life-map, I Ching, and daily guidance outputs.
  • Apple StoreKit purchase and restore information, such as product identifiers and transaction identifiers.
  • Basic technical data needed for security, troubleshooting, and reliable operation.

How we use data

  • To create your personal life map and daily guidance.
  • To generate decision readings, reports, and app experiences based on your saved profile.
  • To process in-app purchases, restore purchases, and reconnect purchased reports.
  • To remember your preferences and improve the app experience.
  • To protect the service from abuse, errors, and repeated failed requests.

Location

Location is optional. Decision Compass only requests location when you tap the current location button. It is used to improve birthplace coordinates and true-solar-time correction. The app does not request background location.

Purchases

iOS purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Decision Compass may receive verified product identifiers, transaction identifiers, purchase dates, and entitlement status so that purchased reports and Restore Purchases can work correctly. We do not receive your full payment card details.

AI-generated content

Decision Compass may use report-generation infrastructure to prepare written reflections from your profile and question context. Outputs are for self-reflection and decision support only. They are not medical, legal, financial, or mental-health advice.

Local data

The app may store profile and daily guidance data locally on your device so the app opens quickly and can refresh in the background. Journal entries are intended as private user notes inside the app experience.

Service providers

We use service providers to operate the app and website, including Apple for App Store purchases, hosting providers for the app API and website, and infrastructure used for report generation, storage, email delivery, and abuse prevention where configured.

Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the app, support purchases and restores, maintain security, comply with legal or accounting obligations, and resolve support requests. You can request deletion of personal data unless we need to keep limited records for legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, or dispute reasons.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or receive a copy of your personal data. To exercise a right, email info@finnexion.com.

Children

Decision Compass is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The latest version will be posted on this page with the updated date.
