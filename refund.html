Purchases, refunds, and restore access

Last updated: 30 June 2026

StoreKit Restore

Decision Compass may offer focused readings, life-map reports, and subscription features through Apple in-app purchases. This page explains how refunds and restore purchases work.

App Store purchases

iOS purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Apple handles payment details, receipts, billing issues, and App Store refund requests.

Restore purchases

If you reinstall the app or move to a new device, use Restore Purchases in the app while signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Restore Purchases is designed to reconnect verified StoreKit purchases and previously generated reports where available.

Refund requests

App Store refunds are requested through Apple. You can still contact Decision Compass support if you need help restoring access or locating a purchased report before starting a refund request.

When support can help

  • A purchase succeeded, but the report did not appear.
  • Restore Purchases completed, but the app did not reconnect expected access.
  • You changed devices and need help checking which Apple ID was used.
  • You need clarification before contacting Apple about a refund.

Product limits

Decision Compass is for self-reflection and decision support. It does not provide medical, legal, financial, or mental-health advice, and readings do not guarantee a specific outcome.
